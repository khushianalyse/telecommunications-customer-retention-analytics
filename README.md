# Telecommunications Customer Retention Analytics Using Python

## Project Overview

Customer churn is a major challenge for telecommunications companies because losing existing customers increases acquisition costs and impacts recurring revenue.

This project performs exploratory data analysis (EDA) on the IBM Telco Customer Churn dataset to identify key factors associated with customer churn and provide data-driven recommendations to improve customer retention.

---

## Business Problem

Telecommunications companies need to understand why customers leave and identify high-risk customer groups.

This analysis aims to answer:

- What percentage of customers are leaving the service?
- Which customer characteristics are associated with higher churn?
- How do contract type, tenure, pricing, internet service, and technical support influence retention?

---

## Project Objectives

The objectives of this project are:

- Clean and validate customer data.
- Perform exploratory data analysis.
- Identify important churn drivers.
- Analyse customer behaviour patterns.
- Develop business recommendations for improving retention.

## Exploratory Data Analysis

The analysis investigated the relationship between customer churn and:

### 1. Overall Customer Churn

Approximately **26.5% of customers had churned**, highlighting customer retention as a significant business challenge.

### 2. Contract Type

Month-to-month customers showed the highest churn rate (~42.7%), while two-year contract customers had the lowest churn rate (~2.8%).

### 3. Customer Tenure

Customers who churned had a lower average tenure (~18 months) compared with retained customers (~38 months).

### 4. Monthly Charges

Customers who churned had higher average monthly charges, suggesting pricing and customer value perception may influence retention.

### 5. Internet Service

Fiber optic customers showed the highest churn rate among internet service categories.

### 6. Technical Support

Customers without technical support experienced significantly higher churn compared with customers receiving support.

---

## Key Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

### Encourage Longer-Term Contracts

Provide incentives and loyalty benefits to encourage customers to move from month-to-month contracts.

### Improve Early Customer Engagement

Strengthen onboarding and engagement strategies during the early customer lifecycle.

### Review Pricing Strategies

Develop personalised retention offers for higher-value customers.

### Improve Fiber Customer Experience

Investigate service quality, pricing, and satisfaction among fiber optic customers.

### Promote Technical Support

Increase awareness and adoption of technical support services.
